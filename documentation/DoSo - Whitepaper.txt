Design The Project
We encounter problems in our everyday lives, but for each problem,
people tend to have countless ideas for solving them. However, just
having an idea is far different from implementing a solution, and
truly solving many problems takes a plan and resources. That's
where ArdaNova comes in.

Anyone can create an account and launch a project on ArdaNova. Our
platform transforms ideas into worker-owned cooperatives where
everyone who contributes becomes a co-owner. A project has two
parts: the definition of a problem and the definition of the
solution.

To define a problem, use our presentation studio. Create compelling
materials that highlight the problem as you see it. Add videos,
images, and icons to help people understand what you're trying to
solve.

Next, define the solution. First with visuals and narrative, then
more concretely with our project management tools: break your vision
into phases, then milestones, then specific tasks. Each task can be
posted to our contributor network, where skilled people can take on
work and earn ownership shares—not just one-time payments.


Get the Support
ArdaNova is not just about sharing ideas. It's about building them
into community-owned organizations with real impact.

After the project is designed, you launch a crowdfunding campaign.
Supporters don't just donate—they become stakeholders. Contributors,
funders, and advocates all receive ownership shares proportional to
their contribution.

Support comes in many forms:
- Financial: Direct funding through the platform
- Labor: Skilled contributors completing tasks for equity
- Resources: Equipment, services, or infrastructure
- Engagement: Spreading the word and building community
- Connections: Introductions, partnerships, and expertise

Stakeholders participate in democratic governance—voting on
decisions, budget allocation, and strategic direction. Those most
invested have the strongest voice, but every member has a seat at
the table.

Once your campaign reaches its funding goal, ArdaNova helps
facilitate the launch of your cooperative. Stakeholders can receive
dividends, access goods and services from the project, or reinvest
their shares into other community projects on the platform.


Get It Done
ArdaNova builds a profile for each user based on their skills,
experience, and interests. Each user has a public page showcasing
their accomplishments, expertise, and portfolio of projects they've
contributed to.

With consent, users join the ArdaNova contributor network—a
marketplace where people are matched to meaningful work based on
their abilities. Unlike traditional freelancing, completing tasks
earns you ownership. Every contributor becomes a co-owner of what
they help build.

For interactive experiences, our developer tools allow apps and
games to integrate with the platform. Users can earn ownership
shares through engagement, which they can hold, exchange, or
reinvest in projects they believe in.

Our project management tools help organizers stay on track—with
templates, task breakdowns, contributor matching, and progress
tracking. All ownership records are transparent and permanently
documented, so everyone can verify their stake.

To support this ecosystem, ArdaNova operates as a cooperative
itself. Platform fees flow into a shared treasury—distributed to
fund new community projects, reward active contributors, cover
operations, and support grants for underserved communities.

Built on modern infrastructure designed for speed, security, and
minimal transaction costs, ArdaNova provides the foundation for a
more cooperative economy—where success is shared by all who
contribute.
