# ArdaNova Product Definition

**ArdaNova** is a decentralized, worker-owned cooperative platform that reimagines the gig economy by integrating gamification, tokenomics, and transparent governance. It empowers freelancers and creators to become owners of the projects they contribute to.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Gamified Talent Marketplace**: Upwork reimagined where work earns not just cash, but equity and reputation.
- **Worker-Owned Cooperatives**: seamless creation and management of DAOs and cooperatives.
- **Circular Economy**: Value flows back to contributors through the `ARDA` token and project-specific equity shares.
- **Transparent Governance**: On-chain decision making for projects and guilds.

## Key Features
- **Projects**: The core unit of work. Can be temporary, long-term, or foundation-based.
- **Guilds**: Collectives of skilled workers (e.g., "React Developers Guild") that can bid on projects or mentor members.
- **Opportunities**: Job listings and tasks that users can apply for.
- **Game SDK**: Integration for play-to-earn mechanics.
- **Gamma API + MCP**: AI-powered project generation and management.

## User Roles
- **Innovator**: Project creators and founders.
- **Contributor**: Freelancers and workers.
- **Supporter**: Investors and community backers.
- **Guild Member**: Part of a specialized skill group.
