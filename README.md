#### DoSo: The Social Network for Doing

---

### **1. Introduction**

**DoSo** is a comprehensive platform designed to empower individuals to transform innovative ideas into tangible solutions and support the growth of the businesses that emerge from these endeavors.  
It provides a structured environment for:

- Defining problems
- Outlining solutions
- Garnering community support
- Securing resources
- Offering essential digital tools for operational success

---

### **2. Problem Statement**

Many individuals possess innovative ideas for solving everyday problems but **lack a structured plan, resources, and community support** necessary for implementation.  
Simultaneously, **SMEs, freelancers, and micro-businesses** face significant operational challenges, including:

- Lack of affordable, localized digital tools for accounting, invoicing, inventory management, marketing automation, and efficient payment collection

**DoSo addresses both these gaps** by uniting ideation, execution, and sustained business growth within a single ecosystem.

---

### **3. Solution: The DoSo Ecosystem**

DoSo operates as a **unified platform** where ideas are nurtured into successful projects and businesses, facilitated by community support, agency involvement, and integrated digital tools.

#### **3.1 Project Design and Support**

- **Design The Project:**  
  - Define a problem and outline a detailed solution
  - Create multimedia presentations (videos, images, icons)
  - Break down tasks, required personnel/skills, and resources
  - Manage everything via a built-in project management tool

- **Get the Support:**  
  Project creators present their ideas to targeted audiences (by industry, location, or social initiative).  
  Supporters can:
    - Cast votes to show belief in the solution
    - Subscribe to projects (with funds awarded monthly)
    - Volunteer to accomplish tasks
    - Apply for roles within the project

#### **3.2 Facilitated Execution and Agency Collaboration**

- **Agency Bidding:**  
  Registered agencies can bid to work on projects, introducing a professional service layer for robust and scalable execution.

- **Centralized Funding:**  
  Platform fees (from SaaS subscriptions & add-ons) are used to help fund supported projects, facilitating partnerships between creators and agencies.

- **Funding Mechanism:**  
  Upon majority support, DoSo awards funds/resources to kickstart projects. In return, DoSo and supporters receive dividend-yielding shares from the new organization.

- **Talent Network:**  
  DoSo collects skill and experience data to build a talent network for potential hiring on funded projects.

- **Supply Chain:**  
  DoSo will develop its own supply chain, leveraging internal projects and partnerships with established companies.

- **Corporate Presence:**  
  A dedicated corporate team (supply chain managers, developers, marketers, consultants) ensures the success and sustainability of DoSo-backed ventures.

#### **3.3 Integrated SME Business Tools**

DoSo offers a suite of digital tools to support projects as they evolve into businesses:

- **Invoicing & Payment Collection**
  - Seamless invoice creation and delivery via WhatsApp/email
  - Multiple payment options: STRIPE

- **Inventory & Sales Management**
  - Real-time stock tracking
  - Sales recording and management
  - Automated alerts for low inventory

- **Marketing Automation**
  - Scheduling WhatsApp & Instagram posts
  - AI content generator for marketing captions/ad copy

- **Business Analytics Dashboard**
  - Daily sales summaries
  - Profit & loss reporting

- **Micro-loan Access (Future Add-on)**
  - Partnerships with fintechs to provide micro-loans for SMEs

---

### **4. Target Audience**

The DoSo ecosystem serves:

- **Individuals with Ideas:** Innovators/problem-solvers seeking to launch projects
- **Agencies:** Professional service providers seeking new opportunities
- **Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs):** Businesses across sectors (hair salons, supermarkets, fashion retailers, POS agents, etc.)
- **Freelancers & Micro-businesses:** Individuals and small operations needing cost-effective, localized solutions

---

### **5. Monetization Strategy**

DoSo’s strategy ensures sustainability and funding for new ventures:

- **Equity from Funded Ventures:**  
  Dividend-yielding shares from successful projects

- **Tiered SaaS Subscription Model for SME Tools:**
    - **Free Plan:** Basic features (e.g., 5 invoices/month)
    - **Starter (₦3,000/month):** Unlimited invoices, inventory management
    - **Pro (₦7,500/month):** Includes marketing automation, analytics dashboard
    - **Enterprise (Custom Pricing):** Tailored solutions for larger/multi-store operations

- **Add-ons:**
    - SMS Campaigns: (₦50/SMS)
    - AI Business Advisor: (₦1,000/month extra)

- **Platform Fees for Agency Bids (Potential):**
    - Small fee for agency bids or project acquisition, contributing to the funding pool

### **6. Technology Stack**

DoSo will leverage a robust technology stack:

- **Fullstack (Web):** React / Next.js
- **Mobile App:** Flutter (cross-platform for iOS & Android)
- **Database:** MySql
- **Payments Integration:** STRIPE
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Vercel + Railway
